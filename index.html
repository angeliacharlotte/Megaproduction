ABOUT US

What is PT. Mega Production Corp?

PT. Mega Production Corp is an upgrade from CV. Mega Production with the same concept and organization. PT Mega Production Corp was established on February 5, 2021, previously operating as a CV, which was founded on July 17, 2013. We are a service-based company operating on a fee contract basis, specializing in Event Organization (Pre-production event, Man Power, and Post-production event), Transportation Rental, Special Metalwork Industry Services, and Media Advertising. We have company branches strategically located in major cities in the Jakarta and West Java areas, allowing us to facilitate our services in distributing ongoing projects.
